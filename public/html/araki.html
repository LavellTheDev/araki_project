Araki Project

The Araki Project is a look at Japanese culture through my eyes, and how I’ve seen the different sides of it in terms of what is traditional and not. My grandmother did not live a traditional Japanese life, and Western views impacted her life greatly. Beyond this story, there are many stories that may not be discussed much through history, and it is my plan to bring light to such events.
