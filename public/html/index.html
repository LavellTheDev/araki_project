Araki Project

The Araki Project is an accumulation of different types of media (Photographs, text, and video). It is partially a look back on the history of my ancestors, and the legacy it left behind, and how it continues to strive today in many forms. It is also about the underbelly of Japanese culture, mental illness, shame, and substance abuse.

Favored Terrain

“The modus operandi for favored terrain is to supply table top gamers with high quality tabletop terrain and accessories to further immersion and spark creativity” -Robert Amaral (founder)

Blog

This blog is made of essays, stories (fiction and non-fiction), and interviews with people of interest. It is about personal and interpersonal communications and how I came to get into the storytelling business, and speaking of my experiences working with Favored Terrain, and also the Araki Project.
